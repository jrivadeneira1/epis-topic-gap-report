Methodological note

This prototype identifies candidate topic gaps by extracting text from EPIS publication PDFs, scoring predefined topic categories, comparing each working group against the corpus average, and filtering results through a relevance matrix.

The results should be treated as candidate gaps for human review, not as final editorial conclusions. A researcher should validate each suggestion before proposing it as an EPIS contribution.
